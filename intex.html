What is ManuSkill About?

Almost 40% of high school graduates do not go to college. Elkhart, IN is a community that leads the nation in manufacturing jobs. Non-college bound students have a lack of guidance when it comes to starting a career. ManuSkill wants to help bring local industry leaders into schools for open presentations about the soft skills of employability.
